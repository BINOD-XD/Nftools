Welcome to **nftools**, a high-performance, commercial-grade web utility designed for both Netflix stream lovers and account providers. This platform serves a dual purpose: providing ready-to-use free Netflix access links and allowing users to convert their own active Netflix cookies into stable, bypass-ready **NFToken** login links.

## 🌟 Key Features & Capabilities

* **🎁 Free Netflix NFToken Access:** Standard users can directly fetch fully active and working Netflix NFToken links from our premium pooled database with a single click.
* **⚙️ Custom Cookie-to-Token Generator:** Paste your own Netflix cookie data, and our backend will instantly process, extract, and generate a fresh, shareable NFToken URL.
* **🛡️ Zero Dead-Cookie Policy:** The system runs a real-time health check on all backend and user-submitted cookies. If an NFToken link is generated, it is 100% guaranteed to be backed by an active, working session.
* **🔄 Advanced App Synchronization:** Generated links are optimized to bypass restrictions and log into the official Netflix mobile/TV apps. In case of initial sync delays, a simple multi-attempt retry guarantees entry.
* **📊 Smart Resource & Quota Management:** Built-in anti-abuse filter that allows free users to safely enjoy a quota of 3 account generations per day. Page refresh mechanics are integrated to dynamically clear cached data.
* **💎 Premium Subscription (Coming Soon):** An upcoming paid subscription tier designed to unlock **unlimited daily generations** for power users and commercial providers.
